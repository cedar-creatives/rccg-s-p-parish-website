Types of Giving

Understanding the different ways you can honour God with your resources.

🙏

Tithe

The tithe is 10% of your income, returned to God as an act of worship and trust. It is the foundation of biblical giving (Malachi 3:10).

🎁

Offering

An offering is a freewill gift given above the tithe — an expression of gratitude and love for what God has done in your life.

🌱

Special Seed

A seed sown in faith for a specific breakthrough, miracle or answer to prayer. God honours faith-filled giving (2 Corinthians 9:6).

🏗️

Building Fund

Contributions specifically towards the construction, renovation and expansion of our church building and facilities.
