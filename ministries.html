Our Ministries

Every member is called to serve. Find your place, use your gifts and make a difference in God's kingdom.

Connect & Serve

Six vibrant ministries — each one a family, each one a calling.

✝️

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry is a vibrant community of young people (ages 13–35) committed to growing in faith, building godly character and impacting their generation for Christ.

What We Do

  • Weekly youth fellowship and Bible study
  • Youth hangouts, retreats and camps
  • Mentorship and discipleship programmes
  • Community outreach and evangelism

Meeting Time

📅 Saturdays • 3:00 PM – 6:00 PM

Join Youth Ministry
⭐

Children Ministry

The Children's Ministry (ages 0–12) creates a safe, fun and faith-filled environment where children learn about God, develop godly values and grow in the knowledge of His Word.

What We Do

  • Sunday school classes during main service
  • Bible memory and storytelling sessions
  • Children's choir and drama
  • Annual children's day celebration

Meeting Time

📅 Sundays • 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM & 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM (concurrent with main service)

Volunteer with Children
🎵

Choir

The Choir leads the congregation into the presence of God through anointed worship and praise. We believe music is a powerful weapon of spiritual warfare and a gateway to divine encounters.

What We Do

  • Lead worship at all Sunday services
  • Special musical performances at events
  • Weekly rehearsals and vocal training
  • Recording and releasing worship content

Rehearsal Time

📅 Saturdays • 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Join the Choir
🤝

Ushering Unit

The Ushering Unit is the first point of contact for every visitor and member. We create a warm, welcoming atmosphere that reflects the love of Christ and ensures orderly, Spirit-filled services.

What We Do

  • Welcome and seat members and visitors
  • Maintain order during services
  • Coordinate offerings and announcements
  • Support event logistics and crowd management

Meeting Time

📅 Sundays • 8:30 AM (pre-service briefing)

Join Ushering Unit
📷

Media Team

The Media Team captures, produces and broadcasts the work of God at RCCG S&P Parish. From photography to live streaming, we ensure the gospel reaches beyond the four walls of the church.

What We Do

  • Photography and videography at services and events
  • Live streaming of Sunday services
  • Social media content creation and management
  • Sermon recording, editing and uploading

Meeting Time

📅 Sundays • 9:00 AM (setup before service)

Join Media Team
📢

Evangelism Team

The Evangelism Team carries the gospel beyond the church walls — to streets, hospitals, schools and homes. We are passionate about souls and committed to the Great Commission.

What We Do

  • Street evangelism and tract distribution
  • Hospital and prison visitation
  • Door-to-door outreach in the community
  • Follow-up and discipleship of new converts

Meeting Time

📅 Last Saturday of every month • 8:00 AM

Join Evangelism Team
