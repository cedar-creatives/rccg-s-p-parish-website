Starting Your Day With God: The Power of Morning Devotion

There is something profoundly transformative about beginning each day in the presence of God. Before the noise of the world rushes in — before the notifications, the demands and the pressures — there is a sacred window of time that belongs entirely to you and your Creator.

The Psalmist declared, "In the morning, Lord, you hear my voice; in the morning I lay my requests before you and wait expectantly" (Psalm 5:3). This was not merely a poetic expression — it was a deliberate, daily discipline that shaped the entire trajectory of his day.
