Mission, Vision & Values

The convictions that shape everything we do.

Our Mission

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ by preaching the gospel, building believers in the Word of God, equipping the saints for ministry, and extending God's kingdom in Lagos and beyond.

Our Vision

Our Vision

  • To make heaven.
  • To take as many people as possible with us.
  • To have a member of R.C.C.G in every family of all Nations.
  • To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  • To plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries, and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  • We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for Jesus Christ Our Lord.
Our Values

Our Values

  • Faith rooted in God's Word
  • Authentic worship & prayer
  • Discipleship & mentorship
  • Community & fellowship
  • Outreach & evangelism

Our Denomination

RCCG S&P Parish is proudly a part of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) — one of the largest and fastest-growing Pentecostal churches in the world, founded in Nigeria in 1952 by Reverend Josiah Olufemi Akindayomi.

Under the General Overseer Pastor Enoch Adeboye, the RCCG has expanded to over 190 countries with tens of thousands of parishes. We carry that same mandate: to make heaven, to take as many people as possible with us, and to have a member of RCCG within five minutes of every person on the face of the earth.
