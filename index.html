About Our Church

Building lives through the power of God's Word.

Growing Together In Christ

Founded on the 17th of January 2009, RCCG S&P Parish is a Christ-centered church committed to raising believers, transforming lives and building a strong spiritual community through worship, prayer and service.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or searching for a church family, you are warmly welcome to worship with us.
