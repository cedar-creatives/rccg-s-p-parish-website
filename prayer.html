You are not alone. Share your need and our prayer team will stand in agreement with you before God.

"Again I say to you, if two of you agree on earth about anything they ask, it will be done for them by my Father in heaven." — Matthew 18:19

Submit Your Prayer Request

Every request is treated with confidentiality and prayed over by our dedicated prayer team.

🙌 Thank you! Your prayer request has been received. Our prayer team will be interceding for you. God bless you!

🕐 When We Pray

  • Daily — Our prayer team prays over all requests every morning at 6:00 AM
  • Tuesday — Requests are lifted during Digging Deep (6:00 PM – 7:30 PM)
  • Thursday — Faith Clinic is dedicated to intercession (6:30 PM)
  • 1st of Month — Hour of Mercy prayer service (6:00 AM)
  • Sunday — Altar call and corporate prayer after service
